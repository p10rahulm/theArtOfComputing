Art of Computing
February 17th and 18th, 2024. IISc Bangalore.
Venue: A. V. Rama Rao Auditorium, Chemical Sciences Building, IISc
Argha Manna
Indian Institute of Technology, Gandhinagar
Drawing in the Science Classroom
Abstract: Exploring the possibility of utilizing comics as a new pedagogical tool in Computer Science and Sciences in general.
Speaker Bio
Prof. Argha Manna is an artist in residence at IIT Gandhinagar. He did his Bachelors from Serampore University in Calcutta. He then completed his Masters from University College of Science and Technology from University of Calcutta in 2009. You can find some of his work at https://drawinghistoryofscience.wordpress.com/about-me/

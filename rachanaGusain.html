Would you buy oranges in higher dimensions?
Abstract: In this talk, we will explore some of the interesting and counterintuitive facts that emerge in higher dimensions. The behavior of familiar shapes like cubes and spheres becomes unusually fascinating as we extend our understanding beyond three dimensions, challenging our geometric intuitions.
